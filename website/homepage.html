Study Better

edyou envisions a future where everybody has access to the best resources and where you are not limited by where you are from or who your parents are. To accomplish this, we develop tools that help you study better everyday, increasing the efficiency and quality of your learning.

More Time Teaching

As a teacher you know how precious time is when teaching a whole class of students. So do we at edyou. Therefore, we have developed tools that help you as a teacher spend less time on preparing learning material, preparing classes and correcting tests. Our goal is to ensure teachers spend less time on tedious tasks not directly relevant to teaching students, and more time on facilitating learning for all their students.

Increase Quality and Efficiency

Our main vision is to create a more efficient learning process through increased usage of digital technology in education. Students and teachers generate massive amounts of data during their activities which can be used to provide valuable insights for both the teacher and the student. This is what we do at edyou, creating the right tools for the future of education.
