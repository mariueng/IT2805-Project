AdaptiveLearn - Learning adapted yo you

Education should adapt to your needs - not the other way around. AdaptiveLearn is the solution to the problem of assuming everybody learns in the way. On the contrary, everybody is different and everybody learns with different pace and style. AdaptiveLearn uses your learning activities to recommend future topics, provide an overview of progress and a summary of your work. In this way, you are able to always know where you are, what you've done, and where you're going.

Insight Analytics - Customize teaching in a new way

InsightAnalytics (IA) is the tool for the teacher of the future. With IA you can visualize your students in a matter of seconds, retrieving critical information and patterns which have never been visible before. The visualization provide you with the tools to adapt the education and teaching to the individual student with minimum effort, resulting in customized learning that maximizes student potential.

CreativeContent - The best resources

CreativeContent (CC) is the marketplace for the best quality learning material. through working with teachers and content creators around the world, we've created the best learning resources to ease the production of course material and preparation. Additionally, the resources are evaluated based on student performance and feedback, so that the content can be adapted to the best possible material for all students and teachers.
