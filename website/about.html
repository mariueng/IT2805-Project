About edyou

The company was started in 2021 as a response to the lack of digital tools for students in high schools and higher education. Over the last 20 years there has been a massive increase in digital tools for educators and students, however, none seem to solve the real problems that students and teachers have. The adoption rate of digital tools in education is far below the rate of development of new solutions, therefore we believe the wrong tools are developed.

At edyou we wish to deliver integratable solutions to solve the problems of teachers students, contrary to digital solutions that introduce uneccesary complexity and overhead. Therefore, our solutions integrate seemlessly with existing LMS platforms, other digital tools and resources to create the most value for effort for both students and teachers.
